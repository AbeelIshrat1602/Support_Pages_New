Is It Covered by My Insurance at Nao Medical?

At Nao Medical, many services are covered by various insurance providers, but coverage ultimately depends on the specifics of your individual insurance plan. We work with most major insurance carriers to ensure our services are as accessible as possible.

However, coverage can vary based on factors including:

  • Your insurance network
  • The type of plan you have
  • Deductibles, copayments, and out-of-pocket maximums
  • The specific services you need (e.g., urgent care, primary care, lab testing)

How to Confirm Insurance Coverage

  1. Contact Nao Medical: Our staff can verify whether we accept your insurance provider and confirm coverage for specific services.
  2. Check With Your Insurance Provider: Confirm with your insurer whether Nao Medical is in-network and check your deductible or copay requirements.
  3. Understand Out-Of-Pocket Costs: Costs such as copays or additional fees may apply depending on your plan.

Need Assistance?

Our team is always happy to assist you in understanding your coverage and providing cost transparency. Feel free to call us or bring your insurance details to your appointment for personalized guidance.
