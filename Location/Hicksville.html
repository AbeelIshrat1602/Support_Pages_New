Location & Hours

Where is Nao Medical Hicksville located?

You can find us at 232 W Old Country Rd, Hicksville, NY 11801.

What are the operating hours?

  • Monday to Friday: 8 AM - 8 PM
  • Saturday to Sunday: 9 AM - 4 PM
  • Holidays: 9 AM - 3 PM

Services Offered

What kind of services does Nao Medical provide?

At our Hicksville location, we offer a wide range of urgent care services, including but not limited to:

  • Medical evaluations
  • Treatment for minor injuries
  • Diagnostics and lab tests
  • Immunizations
  • Preventive healthcare

Do I need an appointment?

While we welcome walk-ins, booking an appointment in advance can save you time! You can easily schedule your visit on our website.

Insurance & Payments

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept a variety of insurance plans. Please check our website or contact our office for specific details regarding your plan.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards, debit cards, and cash.

General Inquiries

Is parking available?

Yes, there is street parking available nearby. However, we recommend public transportation for convenience, as our location is easily accessible by various subway and bus lines.

What should I bring for my visit?

To ensure a smooth experience:

  • Bring your insurance card, if applicable.
  • Have a valid ID ready.
  • Prepare a list of any medications you are currently taking.

Can I visit for a COVID-19 test or vaccination?

Absolutely! We provide COVID-19 testing and vaccination services. Check our website for details on availability and appointment scheduling.
