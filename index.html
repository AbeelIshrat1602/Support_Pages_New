FAQs

What is virtual care for occupational health?

Virtual care for occupational health refers to using telemedicine to provide employees with healthcare services such as wellness checkups, mental health support, and follow-up consultations.

How does virtual care benefit employers?

Employers benefit from reduced downtime, lower healthcare costs, and improved employee satisfaction and retention by offering accessible and convenient health services.

Can virtual care handle workers’ compensation cases?

Yes, our platform includes follow-up consultations for workers’ compensation cases, ensuring employees receive proper care and guidance during recovery.

Is virtual care suitable for remote employees?

Absolutely. Virtual care is designed to support employees wherever they are, making it an ideal solution for remote and hybrid teams.

How do employees schedule virtual care appointments?

Employees can easily book appointments by calling us at (917) 633-0834 or through our websote, choosing a time that fits their schedule.

Does virtual care include mental health services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive virtual mental health support, including counseling and stress management, to promote overall well-being.
